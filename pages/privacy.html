Privacy Policy

This page describes how Salon Public handles information collected through the public website and inquiry flows.

Information we collect

We may collect basic contact details, organization information, and project notes when a visitor submits an inquiry through the public website.

How we use information

Inquiry details are used to review project scope, respond to requests, and prepare follow-up communication related to the requested public website work.

Analytics and performance data

Website analytics may be used to understand traffic patterns, improve page clarity, and maintain SEO and performance quality.

Data retention

Submitted inquiry information should be retained only as long as needed to manage communication, evaluate project fit, and satisfy applicable legal obligations.

Third-party services

If form delivery, analytics, or email routing is connected to external providers, those providers may process visitor data according to their own policies.

Contact for privacy questions

Questions about privacy handling can be directed through the contact page so the request can be reviewed and routed appropriately.
