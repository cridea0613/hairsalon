FAQ

Common questions about service page structure.

Why split treatments into separate service categories?

It matches search intent more closely and lets visitors compare only the details relevant to the treatment they want.

Should every service page include pricing guidance?

Yes. Even a range or starting price reduces confusion and filters low-intent inquiries earlier.

What content helps first-time visitors most?

Duration, maintenance expectations, suitability guidance, and preparation steps do the most work before booking.
